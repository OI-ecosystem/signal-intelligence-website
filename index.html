Common questions
What is Historical Precedent Intelligence?
HPI matches the current signal profile of any live entity against six validated historical cases — SpaceX, Tesla, Bitcoin, NVIDIA, Shopify, and OpenAI. It identifies which historical trajectory the current entity most resembles and calculates a maturity window showing where in the cycle it likely sits.
How is the Pokémon newsletter different from general trading advice?
It is not advice. It is structured signal intelligence — inventory compression data, PSA population scarcity signals, auction acceleration patterns, and market momentum scores — presented as ranked opportunities with full evidence breakdowns. What you do with that information is your decision.
How often are signals updated?
The platform runs continuous collection cycles across all domains. Crypto signals update every 15 minutes. AI and GitHub signals every 30–60 minutes. Collectibles signals daily. The full opportunity engine re-scores after every pipeline run.
Can I access Signal Intelligence via API?
API access is available on the Enterprise plan. It provides programmatic access to signals, entities, CIA scores, opportunity rankings, and HPI analyses. Documentation available on request.
What domains are currently live?
Collectibles, Crypto & DeFi, and AI & Startups are live today. Watches, Property, and Insurance Intelligence are in development and will be added without requiring plan changes for existing subscribers.
