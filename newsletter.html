Common questions
How often is the newsletter published?
Every Monday morning. Each issue covers the full week of signal activity, updated rankings, and watchlist changes for any cards that crossed detection thresholds during the week.
Is this investment advice?
No. Signal Intelligence is an information service. The newsletter presents structured signal data, historical pattern analysis, and opportunity scores. Nothing published constitutes financial, investment, or trading advice. What you do with the information is your decision entirely.
What data sources are analysed?
The Signal Intelligence platform collects from multiple independent sources including marketplace listings, sales data, population reports, auction results, and collector community signals. Every opportunity in the newsletter is cross-source validated before inclusion.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. There are no contracts and no minimum commitment. You can cancel your subscription at any time and you will not be charged again after cancellation. Access continues until the end of your current billing period.
Will historical interpretation be added to this newsletter?
Genesis — our historical interpretation layer — is in active development. It will launch as a separate, premium upgrade above the newsletter, not as an automatic addition to existing subscriptions. Newsletter subscribers will be able to upgrade to Genesis once it's available.
How do I access previous issues?
All issues are delivered by email and remain in your inbox. A subscriber archive is in development and will be available to all active subscribers when released — at no additional cost.
